Who in the heck am I?

I am a graphic artist, creative writer, and I also happen to have a passion for computer programing. This might seem like an extremely weird conglomeration of stuff. But trust me, if you want to study something that can be presented to you as a dry, uninteresting, logical set of rules, codes, and endless dreary syntax, then the last thing that you will probably want is some person that eats, sleeps, and breathes just computer programming. You want a creative writer, and perhaps someone who also dapples in art, because that is probably the way you want to approach things anyway. And that is what you will get, logic and syntax written in a way that only a creative artist and writer, could bring you.
